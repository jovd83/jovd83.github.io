My prompts & agent(skill)s

Over time, I have built a collection of prompts, agents, and agentskills that I use regularly in my work. These resources support analysis, testing, exploration, and structured thinking with AI systems.

They are not tricks or shortcuts. They are patterns that help make interactions with AI more explicit and more repeatable.

On this page, I share some of them openly on my GitHub for reuse and adaptation.
